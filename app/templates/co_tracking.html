Tracking Definitions
The system uses three levels of tracking labels.
Record Statuses

Labels from the uploaded record or remarks: Enrolled, Transfer-In, Pending Transfer-In, and Transfer-Out.

Progression Results

Labels detected by comparing LRNs across years: Regular, Completed, Completed - Irregular, Repeater, and Incomplete.

Review Flags

Signals for school personnel to verify: missing records, transfer concerns, or incomplete progression.
